About

This project was started to help our healtcare centers be more efficient in their daily activities. The long wait times and general miscommunication that often occur in most of our healtcare centers have been a source of great pain to a lot of people.
Parma is a project that has its start as an ALX/HOLBERTON school project.We have aims of growing as a software solution and seeing a mass adoption of PARMA in our healtcare centers.
